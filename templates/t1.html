NER service

This named entity recognition service for Dutch is based on conditional random fields and other machine learning techniques.

For now the training is based on the fairly standard CoNLL 2002 corpus but a more extensive training based on the SoNaR v1.2 is on its way.

The REST service is very simple:

/ner/[your sentence]

For instance, the sentence

Jochim Bloemental is op weg naar Utrecht aangevallen door een bende holigans van Microsoft.

will return the named entities:

  • Joachim Bloemental: Person
  • Utrecht: Location
  • Microsoft: Organization
